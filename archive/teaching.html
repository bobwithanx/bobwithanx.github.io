Teaching Experience

In 2011, I founded Sailbear Labs, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to helping kids ages 12-18 express creativity through filmmaking and storytelling. I serve as Executive Director and lead the workshops and events.

I’ve been an instructor at the Art Institute of Phoenix since 2008, teaching a number of courses in both the Digital Film + Video Production and the Web Design + Interactive Media degree programs. In Fall 2011, I received the Dean’s Award for my teaching efforts during the quarter.

Courses I regularly teach include:

  • Video Production and Lighting Techniques
  • Digital Visual Composition
  • Advanced Interactive Design
  • Principles of Photography
  • User-Centered Design
  • Usability Testing

In 2010, I was a long term substitute at Metro Arts High School and had the opportunity to teach beginning and advanced film courses to some talented students. It led me to obtain my AZ CTEP Teaching Certification and inspired me to start Sailbear Labs.
